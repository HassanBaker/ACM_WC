Predict The World Cup

About

Is artificial intelligence smarter (AI) than an Octopus? At the 2010 FIFA World Cup, Paul the Octopus predicted 12 out of 14 match results correctly but can AI do better in 2018? Researchers at the Insight National Centre for Data Analytics and the UCC student’s chapter of the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) are a 3 week competition period where we encourage participants to build their own predictor using whatever dataset and method they want. At the end of challenge period, participants will submit their predictor code and predictions for every match including knockout stages on or before Jun 14th 2018. A scoring system has been designed to evaluate the effectiveness of the predictors and the winners will announced after the world cup finals.
